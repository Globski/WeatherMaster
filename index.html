About WeatherMaster

WeatherMaster was born out of a passion for weather tracking. As an outdoor enthusiast, I saw the need for a streamlined and ad-free weather application that provides accurate data without the distraction of advertising. This project aims to serve people who need reliable weather information for planning daily activities, events or travel.
